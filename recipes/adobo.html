Chicken Adobo

Description

Adobo is a classic Filipino dish made with chicken or pork marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices. It is known for its savory and slightly tangy flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with soy sauce, vinegar, minced garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken along with the marinade.
  3. Cook the chicken until it is browned on all sides.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the chicken is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Season with salt to taste, and adjust the sauce consistency by adding a little water if necessary.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice.
